How to prepare egg gravy

ingredients:

  1. 4 eggs,boiled
  2. 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  3. 1 tsp red chilli powder
  4. 1/2 tsp turmeric power
  5. 1/2 tsp saltd

prepare egg

step 1.Take boiled egg in a bowl
step 2.add ginger garlic-paste red-chilli powder,turmeric powder,salt, cumin powder and coriander powder
step 3.mix the spices throughly
step 4.take a pan of oil,put the eggs in the pan and saute

prepare gravy:

step 1.take a pan and add cumin seeds,green chilli and chopped onion
step 2.saute them till golden brown and then add ginger,garlic,turmeric powder,corinder powder,cumin powder.red chilli poweder and salt
step 3.mix them well
step 4.add water and let it simmer

step 6.grind the mixture to get the perfect mix oif spicy gravy
